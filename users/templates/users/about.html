About Green Leaf

Welcome to GreenLeaf, a platform designed to simplify the process of testing and assessing knowledge for mentors and managers. Our objective is to provide an easy-to-use platform that empowers mentors to gauge the skill levels of their mentees or employees, enabling them to assign appropriate tasks and track progress effectively.
At GreenLeaf, mentors have the ability to create and upload custom questions tailored to the specific technologies they are teaching. This allows mentees to attempt these questions and showcase their understanding. By assessing the mentees' proficiency and skill levels, mentors can assign tasks that are challenging yet suitable for individual growth.
One of the key features of our platform is the common leaderboard. It serves as a hub for mentors and mentees to track and compare the performance of all participants. Mentees can strive for a better rank among their peers, fostering healthy competition and encouraging continuous improvement.
We understand the challenges faced by mentors when managing quiz results manually. The task is not only time-consuming but also prone to errors in calculation and tracking of questions and answers. To address this challenge, we have developed an online quiz system as part of our Minimum Viable Product (MVP1). With GreenLeaf, you can say goodbye to the margin of error associated with manual quiz result management.
Join us at GreenLeaf and experience a seamless, error-free platform that streamlines the testing and assessment process for mentors and managers.
